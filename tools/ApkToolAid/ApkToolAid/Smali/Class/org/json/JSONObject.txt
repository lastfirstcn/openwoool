A modifiable set of name/value mappings. Names are unique, non-null strings. Values may be any mix of JSONObject, JSONArray, Strings, Booleans, Integers, Longs, Doubles or NULL. Values may not be null, Double#isNaN(), Double#isInfinite(), or of any type not listed here.
